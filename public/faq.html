F.A.Q.

What are Blast Notifications?
Blast Notifications are real time email updates with links to new web content.

How soon after a website uploads content will I receive a notification?
You should receive a notification within a 0-10 or 0-15 minute window depending on the notification.

Can I unsubscribe from Blast Notifications?
You can unsubscribe from all or individual Blast Notifications at any time by emailing contact@njweb.solutions with your request.
